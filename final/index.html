Welcome

The purpose of this website is to serve as a digital exhibit of Ottawa, Carleton University, ARC, ms. 1. This manuscript is a chant book discovered in the Archives of Carleton University, and has been studied extensively by students in the History Department. In the age of the internet and online sharing, manuscripts are being digitized in order to allow better access to these old texts, as well as to help further preserve a manuscript in its natural state. With the discovery of ARC ms. 1 at Carleton University, there is a push to make the manuscript accessible to researchers of old chant books as well as to entice students to take an interest in the middle ages. This website will present this manuscript to an audience in a way that is interactive, allowing users to look at images of this manuscript as well as read up on the research that was done on the manuscript prior to the creation of the website.
